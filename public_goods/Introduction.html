Introduction

In this game with {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP}} players, each player starts with an endowment of {{C.ENDOWMENT}}. You must decide whether to keep the money for yourself or contribute it to a common pool. The efficiency factor of {{C.EFFICIENCY}} means that for every point contributed to the common pool, it generates {{C.EFFICIENCY}} units of benefit to all players. The challenge is to balance individual gains with maximizing the overall benefit for everyone.
